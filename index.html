About Us

Our Love Story

Jack and Jill's love story began in Africa, where they selflessly volunteered for a well-known charity, working together to build schools, bridges, and wells for communities in need. On one fateful day, as Jack ascended the hills to fetch water from a well, an unexpected stumble caused him to come tumbling into Jill's path. In that moment, their eyes met, and an instant connection ignited. From that day forward, they became inseparable, bound by a love that was as spontaneous as it was deep. Together, they continue to embark on a journey of shared adventures, supporting each other through life's challenges, and cherishing the remarkable love story that unfolded on that memorable day in Africa.

Our Engagement

Jack and Jill's love story reached a beautiful milestone when Jill decided to propose to Jack at their cherished hilltop, a place that overlooked the vast sea and their beloved town. However, as the words escaped Jill's lips, a wave of surprise washed over Jack, causing him to stumble and accidentally bump his head. In that moment, amidst the shock and laughter, Jack joyfully accepted Jill's heartfelt proposal and made a promise to be her loving caretaker for all eternity. This extraordinary moment sealed their bond, forever intertwining their lives with a shared commitment to love and care for each other throughout their journey.
